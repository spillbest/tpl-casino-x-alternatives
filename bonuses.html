Types of casino bonus

Welcome match

A percentage match on the first deposit, usually the main conversion offer.

Free spins

Slot spins attached to a deposit or registration bonus, often with separate wagering.

No-deposit bonus

A smaller trial offer that lets players test the operator before depositing.

Reload bonus

A returning-player offer that adds value after the first deposit.

Cashback

A percentage of losses returned over a defined period, sometimes with lower wagering.

VIP rewards

Loyalty tiers, points, personal rewards and faster service for regular players.

Understanding wagering requirements

What does 35x wagering mean?

The player must place bets equal to 35 times the bonus amount before bonus winnings can be withdrawn.

Do all games count equally?

No. Slots often count more than table or live games. Always check game weighting before claiming.

Is lower wagering better?

Usually yes. A smaller headline bonus with lower wagering may be easier to clear than a huge bonus with strict rules.

How should a player claim a code?

Enter the code in the registration or cashier flow, then confirm the final terms on the operator site.
